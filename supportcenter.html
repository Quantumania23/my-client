Support Center

How can I contact TKE Consultancy for support?

You can contact our support team by filling out the form on our Contact Us page or by sending an email to support@tkeconsultancy.com. Our team will respond to your queries and provide the necessary assistance.

What are the business hours for TKE Consultancy's support?

Our support team is available to assist you from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (local time). Please note that our support services may be limited during weekends and public holidays.

Can I request a refund for TKE Consultancy services?

Refund policies may vary depending on the type of service you have availed from TKE Consultancy. Please refer to our Terms & Conditions or contact our support team for more information regarding refund requests.

How can I provide feedback or suggestions to TKE Consultancy?

We welcome your feedback and suggestions. You can share your thoughts by sending an email to feedback@tkeconsultancy.com or by filling out the feedback form on our website. Your feedback helps us improve our services and better meet the needs of our clients.
